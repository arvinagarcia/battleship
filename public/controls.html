Placement

Use the grid to place your fleet. You have five ships of varying sizes: Carrier (5 cells), Battleship (4 cells), Cruiser (3 cells), Submarine (3 cells), and Destroyer (2 cells). Ships can be placed vertically or horizontally but cannot overlap or extend beyond the grid.

Gameplay

Players alternate turns, taking one shot per turn by selecting a cell on their opponent's grid. If your shot hits a ship, the cell is marked as a hit. If your shot misses, the cell is marked as a miss. A ship is sunk when all the cells it occupies are hit. The first player to sink all of their opponentâ€™s ships wins the game!

Controls

Drag your ships to the grid to place them and select cells to attack. To rotate a ship, select a ship and press the Rotate button or the R key on your keyboard.
